Your Shopfront — brand mark inspection

What to look for, in order: readability of the mark at 16 px favicon size; cobalt counter visibility at 24 px+; balance of the stacked-rooflines crossbar (the two horizontal shingles inside the A — they should read as separate strokes, not muddy together); kerning of the wordmark at the header / footer / hero sizes (no awkward gaps after the “A”); contrast on every background, especially the dark cobalt and pure black rows.

Variants tested: apex-mark.svg (full color, ink + cobalt counter), apex-mark-mono.svg (single-color ink, fallback for tight contexts), apex-wordmark.svg (mark + “Your Shopfront” text), apex-logo-square.svg (1024×1024 logo with paper background — used for logo.png).

If anything reads wrong, tell Claude — quick swap to one of the alternate variants (notched A, rule-A) and a re-export takes ~5 min. Otherwise delete this file before merge: rm public/brand/inspect.html · this page is dev-tooling only.
