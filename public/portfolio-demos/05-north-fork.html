↗ The taproom

THE TAPROOM
ON FRANKLIN ST.

Our taproom shares a wall with the brewhouse. Concrete floors, salvaged wood bar, fourteen taps, no tvs, and a kitchen that does one thing — pretzels & a sausage plate.

We open Wednesday through Sunday at four. Food trucks Friday and Saturday. Free pours of whatever's left over, on Sunday afternoons, until we're empty.

↗ Hours & visit

OPEN WED—SUN.
SHUT MON & TUE.

The brewhouse runs Monday and Tuesday. The taproom opens Wednesday at four and stays open until midnight on weekends. 21+ inside, dogs welcome on the patio.

Cash & card. Food trucks rotate weekly — schedule on our Instagram. We don't take reservations except for groups of eight or more.
