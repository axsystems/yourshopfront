How it works

A process built
around your week.

Four touchpoints, all of which take less than a minute. The rest of the time, your laundry is moving and you're not.

Start your first pickup →
i.

Schedule a pickup window

Tell us when. Two-hour windows, seven days a week, including evenings. We text you 30 minutes before your valet arrives.

~ 45 sec
ii.

Hand off the bag

Use any bag for your first pickup. After that, we leave you our reusable Fold bag — branded, oversized, lightly scented like nothing offensive.

~ 30 sec
iii.

We wash, dry, and fold

Sorted by color. Treated with PERC-free, fragrance-free detergent. Folded the way a hotel folds, not the way a college student folds.

~ 18 hours
iv.

We bring it back, fresh

Returned in the next 24—48 hours, in a Fold bag, in the time window you choose. You will smell it before you see it.

~ 30 sec
