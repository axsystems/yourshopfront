🌿 HOW IT WORKS

From quote to green grass
in about a week.

We do most of our scheduling by text. No phone-tag, no awkward "we'll be there sometime Tuesday." You get a real time window the night before.
1

Free quote

Drop your address into the form. We pull satellite imagery to size your lawn and send a flat monthly quote within 24 hours — no on-site visit needed for most yards.

2

Soil test & plan

Before the first visit, we pull soil samples and build a feed program for your specific grass type — Bermuda, St. Augustine, Zoysia, mixed. You get the test results.

3

Same crew, every visit

You meet your two-person crew on day one and see the same faces every two weeks. They know your gate, your dog, and which side of the driveway to leave the trimmer line.

4

Off-season check-ins

Through the dormant months we still drop in monthly — a walk-through, an email update with photos, and any pre-spring prep your soil needs. Always included.
