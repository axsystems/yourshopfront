THE WARRANTY

If it peels, cracks,
or fails — we're back.

  • Two years, written. We hand you the warranty card with the keys. No fine print, no exclusions, no "you used the wrong cleaner."
  • We come back free. Anything that fails in those two years — peeling, blistering, cracking — we re-prep and re-paint, no charge.
  • Touch-up paint included. Every project ends with labeled jars of leftover paint, by room, in case you nick a wall.
  • Same crew, same standard. The crew that painted it is the crew that warranties it. No ticket-routing, no excuses.
  • Backed by $2M liability. Heritage has carried full general & workers' comp insurance for seventeen years. Certificates available on request.
