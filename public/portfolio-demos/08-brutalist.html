[03] / HOW IT GOES

WE WORK FAST
LOUD, &
HONEST.

We take six projects a year. Each engagement runs ten to sixteen weeks. We don't pitch — if you want us to make something for you, you'll know in our first conversation whether we're a fit.

We charge flat fees, not hourly, not retainers. We share rough budgets in the first call. We don't do change orders unless the brief actually changed.

We will ask annoying questions early so we can stop asking them later. Most of our process happens in week one — by week four you should already see something you can show your CEO without flinching.

1

The hello call

30 minutes, no slides, your project & ours. We tell you if we're a fit by minute 25.

2

The proposal, in three pages

Scope, fee, milestones. No "discovery phase." No padding. Two contract signatures.

3

Annoying-question week

One week of fast research, sharp interviews, and the questions you didn't expect.

4

Three directions, big swings

We never bring you safe options. We bring three things you can pick & defend.

5

Build & ship loudly

System, applications, launch assets, social pack. Plus a noisy launch playbook.
