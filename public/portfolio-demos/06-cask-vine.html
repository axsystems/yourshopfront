PHOTOGRAPH · ELENA, BEHIND THE BAR · 11 PM, FRIDAY
The person pouring

Elena Marchetti

Elena opened Cask & Vine in 2019, after eight years on the floor at Bâtard, Frenchette, and a small enoteca in Friuli that she still won't tell anyone the name of. She runs the bar herself, every night, with one person on plates.

"I want this to feel like the back room at a friend's house in somewhere quiet — Trieste, maybe. Twenty-two seats is enough. Eighty-six bottles is enough. The list is short on purpose, so we can know every wine on it."

She also writes a quarterly letter about what she's drinking and which growers are visiting. Our regulars get it first.
