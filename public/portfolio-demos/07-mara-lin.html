I make pictures, mostly. Slow ones.

I'm interested in the moment before the moment — the breath before the sentence, the look between two people who know each other well. My pictures are quiet on purpose.

Trained at SVA (BFA, '13) and as an assistant to Tim Walker and Ryan McGinley between 2014 and 2018. I shoot on a Hasselblad medium format, sometimes a 1973 Pentax 6x7 if the project asks for it, and I direct on 16mm whenever I can. Film holds light differently.

"I'm not interested in beauty. I'm interested in the small, true thing that happens when someone forgets the camera is there." — Mara, in conversation with Apartamento, 2024

I work mostly between New York and Los Angeles, with occasional projects in Mexico and Tokyo. I take three big jobs a year. I take small ones sometimes too, if the room is right.
