📷 Before · Beacon Hill
This week's project

A 1923 craftsman in
Beacon Hill, finally
warm in winter.

The owners had lived with cold rooms upstairs and a furnace that ran nineteen hours a day for the better part of a decade. We replaced the gas furnace with a Mitsubishi cold-climate heat pump, added a single-zone mini-split to the converted attic, and rebalanced the existing duct returns. The whole project took six working days. The annual gas bill dropped by 71%.
