心 Our practice

Quiet rooms.
Long exhales.
No mirrors.

Still Point opened in 2018 with one teacher and one room. We do not have music, we do not have mirrors, we do not have an Instagram presence to speak of. We have twelve mats, eight teachers, a tea kettle, and a small library by the door.

We believe the best yoga is the kind you forget you're doing — where the room dissolves and you find yourself, after sixty minutes, breathing more slowly than when you arrived. That is the only metric we care about.

"We don't want to be the most popular studio in Brooklyn. We want to be the right studio for the hundred and twenty people who practice with us."

Our classes are capped at twelve. We don't accept walk-ins after class begins. Arrive five minutes early, even if you've come a thousand times. The quiet starts in the lobby, not on the mat.
