Practice

A small set of
things, made well.

We are not a full-service agency. We are a film studio that occasionally designs identities. The two disciplines flow into each other in ways we find generative — but the practice is, by intention, narrow.

— 01

Brand films

Three-to-eight-minute films that act as the centerpiece of a brand campaign or chapter launch. Directed in-house, shot on film when the budget allows, scored by composers we trust.

Direction Cinematography Score Color
— 02

Moving identity

Identity systems built from the ground up to live in motion — wordmarks that breathe, logos that resolve over time, type that behaves like a character. We work alongside your existing brand team or as the brand team.

Identity Type Motion Brand strategy
— 03

Campaign direction

Multi-piece advertising campaigns where the through-line is a single directorial sensibility. Hero film, social cuts, OOH photography, brand poster series. One studio, one voice, four months.

Concept Production Photography Print
— 04

Sound & scoring

Original score, sound design, and audio branding — produced in our Tokyo studio. Increasingly, the most important work we do. The image gets remembered because the sound was right.

Composition Foley Mix Audio identity
