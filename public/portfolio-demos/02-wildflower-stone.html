— Flower subscriptions

Flowers delivered
to your home,
quietly, weekly.

Three quiet flower programs. Choose how often, how generous, and whether to leave them at the door or have us hand them off in person.

Cancel any time, with one tap. Pause for travel. Skip a week if your kitchen counter is full. We treat subscriptions like a long, easy friendship.

i.

The weekly bouquet

One hand-tied bouquet, every Friday morning. Reliable, modest, surprisingly transformative.

$48/week
 ii.

The fortnightly arrangement

A larger, vase-finished arrangement every other Friday. Includes vase exchange & care.

$78/biweekly
 iii.

The seasonal wreath

Four wreaths a year, hand-wrapped on grapevine, marking each turn of the season.

— Weddings & events

Wedding flowers,
done quietly.
