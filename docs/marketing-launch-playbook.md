## The math (read first)

| Channel | Realistic conversion | What 10 sales needs |
|---|---|---|
| Cold DM / cold email | 0.2–2% close rate | 500–5,000 contacts |
| LinkedIn DM (warmer) | ~10% reply · ~2% close | ~500 sends |
| Facebook group post | 0.1–0.5% of views convert | 2,000–10,000 views |
| Personal text to warm contacts | 5–20% close | 50–200 contacts |

**Honest call:** 10 cold-organic sales in 1 day is aggressive. Realistic ceiling with hard effort is 3–8. To hit 10, the warm network has to carry volume — existing AZ Window Shine + Spiff Pros customer lists, family/friends, anyone you know who runs a small business. Plan for 5; celebrate at 10.

## Your unfair advantage

You have **30 industry-specific demos**. Don't pitch "I'll build you a website." Pitch **"I already built a demo of YOUR business — here's the link, $99 to make it yours."** Demo-first pitches convert 3–5× higher than generic pitches.

## Channel priority (ranked by ROI for one day)

### 1. Personal-network text blast — highest hit rate
Text every business owner / aspiring business owner in your phone. Conversion typically 10–30%.

**Script:**
```
Hey [name] — just launched Your Shopfront. We do done-for-you
websites for small businesses, live in 24h. Running a promo this
week, $99 setup + $99/mo for 3 months (normally $299 + $149/mo).
Cancel anytime, 30-day money back.

Here's a demo: yourshopfront.com/demos/ironside-plumbing
(Every demo is real and buyable.)

Worth a look for you or anyone you know?
```

Send to 30–50 contacts. Expect 3–8 sales from this alone.

### 2. Cold DM to local AZ trades — volume play
Pull 100 local plumbers / electricians / HVAC / painters / cleaners / roofers from **Google Maps**. Filter for ones with **bad or no websites** (your wedge). Match each to the closest demo.

**Script:**
```
Hi [Name] — saw [Business Name] and noticed you don't have a site
(or your current one is dated). I run Your Shopfront and built a
demo of what a [industry] site looks like in our system:

yourshopfront.com/demos/ironside-plumbing

$99 launch promo this week — live in 24h, $99/mo for 3 months,
cancel anytime. Want to grab one before the promo ends?
```

Channels (in order):
1. Facebook Page Messenger — highest deliverability
2. LinkedIn DM — ~10% reply rate (highest of cold channels)
3. Instagram DM — lower but possible

100 personalized DMs → realistic 1–3 sales day-1, more in the week.

### 4. Nextdoor "Neighbor Services" post

3.2M SMB owners on Nextdoor; home services lead at 37% of business pages. You don't need business verification — use the "Neighbor Services" option.

Post in your neighborhood + nearby. Same value-first frame as Facebook.

### 5. Short-form video — TikTok / IG Reels / YT Shorts

30-second screen-record scrolling through 3–4 demos with text overlay:
- "Every small business in Arizona deserves a real website."
- "$99 launch promo. Live in 24 hours."
- "yourshopfront.com"

Cross-post to all 3 platforms. Video drives 2.5× engagement vs static.

### 6. LinkedIn personal post + targeted DMs

Post your launch story to your personal feed. DM 20 connections who own/work at home-service SMBs. LinkedIn DMs ~10% reply rate.

### 7. Reddit — last priority, high ban risk

r/smallbusiness allows self-promo only in pinned "Self-Promotion Saturday" threads. r/Entrepreneur same. Outside those: comment helpfully on other posts, mention Your Shopfront only if directly relevant. Don't lead with the pitch.

## Hour-by-hour playbook for today

### Hour 0–2 — Prep
- Pull 100-target list from Google Maps (plumbers/electricians/HVAC/painters/cleaners in Phoenix)
- Match each target to the closest demo slug
- Draft 3 message templates (FB DM, LinkedIn DM, text)
- Compile personal-contact list (30–50 people)

### Hour 2–4 — Warm network
- Text the 30–50 personal contacts (highest conversion)
- Call 3–5 closest contacts who run SMBs personally — these close fastest

### Hour 4–10 — Cold blitz
- DM 100 local businesses on Facebook Messenger
- Post in 6 AZ Facebook groups (above)
- Post 1 Nextdoor Neighbor Services
- Post LinkedIn announcement + DM 20 connections
- Record + post 30s demo Reel to TikTok / IG / YT Shorts

### Hour 10–18 — Follow up + close
- Respond to every DM/comment FAST (speed-to-lead = 5× conversion)
- Warm leads: "Promo ends [tomorrow] — want me to start your build tonight?"
- Undecided: send a 2nd personalized demo

### Hour 18–24 — Last push
- Cold-call 20 unresponded high-priority targets (your AZ Window Shine phone experience transfers — same vibe works for SMB owners)

## Multiplier no competitor has

You operate AZ Window Shine, an established Arizona business. Email/text your existing customers:

```
I just launched a sister business that builds websites for small
biz. If you know a plumber/electrician/anyone running their own
thing — I'd love to send them a demo. $99 promo this week.
```

Customer referrals close at 20–40% vs cold's 0.2–2%.

## What to skip today

- Paid Facebook/Google ads (you said organic)
- Cold email at scale — needs warmed domain + verified list; conversion math doesn't work at $99/mo per channel (avg contract <$2K/mo and cold-email economics break)
- Product Hunt / Hacker News — wrong audience for home-service SMBs
- Yelp / Craigslist — too slow / low-intent for day-1
